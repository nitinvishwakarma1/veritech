Contact Panthera

Thank you for your interest in the global movement to save wild cats. Whether you’re here to learn more about wild cats or want to get involved in protecting them, Panthera’s staff is here to help. We receive many inquiries from cat lovers around the world every day, and we do our best to answer every inquiry. We have a small group of support staff and our scientists are (thankfully!) often in the field, so we appreciate your patience..

Panthera USA

With our supporters around the world, we inform the public about the plight of wild cats, work closely with communities living alongside these iconic species, protect wild cats and their prey from poaching, fight the illegal wildlife trade and safeguard the precious landscapes wild cats need to survive.

If you love wild cats, then you’re in our pride. Welcome to the movement to protect these captivating creatures and secure them for future generations.
